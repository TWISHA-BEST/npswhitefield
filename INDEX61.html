school achivements

Inter-Class Competitions - Prize Distribution

The reward for work well done is the opportunity to do more! This was the sentiment that echoed through the auditorium as the students from classes 1 to 5 were felicitated for their glorious achievements in various inter-class competitions. The program commenced with our Honorable Principal Ma’am Dr. Reetu Thapa giving away the prizes for our melodious singers. A promising lot who are truly blessed with great potential! The children of classes 1 and 2 were awarded certificates for interclass poetry, story-telling, solo singing and drawing competition by our Head Academics Ms. Shirin Jeykar. Moving on with the felicitation of our little poets, magnanimous speakers and enigmatic dancers who were warmly complimented by our respected Senior Coordinator Ms. Ritu Kundu, who awarded the certificates to our beaming achievers. For our little painters who have the world as their canvas, we had our very experienced and revered Mentor Principal Ms. Lakshmi Rao who gave away the prizes for our young painters for the inter-class drawing competition. program was concluded with not just cheerful awardees but also many more promises that were silently made in the hearts of the children who were all set to see themselves on stage the coming year! .

Ranked No. 1 School in Times of India School Survey

NPS Whitefield has been ranked No. 1 School in Emerging School Category by Times of India It give us great pleasure to announce that National Public School Whitefield has been ranked No. 1 in the emerging school category by Times of India School Survey. NPS Whitefield is the only school that features in the ranking in the very first year of its inception – a testament to the high standards of educational excellence being followed under the guidance of Dr. K. P. Gopalkrishna, Chairman and Founder of National Public School of Institutions, Directors who are the alumni of prestigious NPS Indiranagar and the Senior Leadership Team comprising of teachers and administrators who come with a rich experience from the parent school, NPS Indiranagar. We aim to continue the tradition of educational excellence by imparting pedagogy in the trans-disciplinary and enquiry learning modes.

celebrations

  • independence day celebrations

    Independence Day was celebrated with patriotic fervor by Pre-Primary students and teachers during the connect time. The children were attired in the colors of the Indian flag and proudly wore badges of our flag carefully crafted by them. Some of them narrated how they celebrated the Independence Day by singing patriotic songs and making our national flag at home. They listened attentively to the story of our freedom movement and were enthralled by the puppet show they had seen. They actively participated in speaking about our national symbols and discussed the importance of freedom. They also were happy to see the surprise video sent by the Pre- Primary teachers which depicted the unity and diversity of our nation. The celebrations concluded with great pride saluting to our mother land. Jai Hind!

  • Class 5 Assembly on the theme ‘Global Family Day’

    The family is the centre of life and it is the key to eternal happiness.”- L. Tom PerryGlobal Family Day is celebrated on January 1st of every year. This day is celebrated as the global day of peace and sharing. Class 5 students discussed the importance of spending and sharing time with their family members for a day. They talked about the UN General Assembly’s declaration in 1997, which was based on the theme, ‘One Day of Peace’. Students highlighted thought provoking ideas like, ‘Focusing just on our family is selfishness, Focusing on Global Family is humanity.’
